The Gerrit Rietveld exhibition in Kiev is a very important event for Ukrainians. After the Ukrainian victory in the Ukrainian-Russian war, the city needs to be rebuilt and reconstructed after the damage inflicted on the city by the Russian army. Ukrainian designers collaborating with designers from Europe are inspired by the style of Gerrit Rietveld and i ntend to adopt some aspects of the style when rebuilding bombed out schools and universities. The exhibition will feature famous works of the famous designer, namely furniture that was revolutionary in its time, as well as updated versions of Gerrit Rietveld's works for modern design. You have a unique opportunity to see the work of Gerrit Rietveld, which are the world classics of furniture and architectural design. And contribute to the reconstruction of Kiev after the cruel war, so all the funds received from the sale of tickets will be allocated for the restoration of infrastructure of the city. All funds will be transferred to the Ministry of Infrastructure of Ukraine.
